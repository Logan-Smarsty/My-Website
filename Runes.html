Runes

Precision

Lethal Tempo

After damaging an enemy champion, Lethal Tempo will begin charging, and after waiting a set amount of time, you'll gain a massive boost to your attack speed. In addition, it allows you to break the hard 2.50 attack speed cap. The duration of the buff is normally 3 seconds, but if you attack an enemy champion during that time, the buff will extend to 6 seconds.

Fleet Footwork

Moving and basic attacking generates Energize stacks, up to 100. When fully Energized, your next basic attack heals you for 10-130 (+5% ability power) (+0.1 bonus attack damage) and grants 20% bonus movement speed for 1 second (depending on level).

Conqueror

Dealing damage to enemy champions grants stacks of Conqueror, lasting for 5 seconds, refreshing on subsequent damage against champions, and stacking up to 12 times. Gain [2 for Melee | 1 for Ranged] stacks from basic attacks and 2 stacks from abilities, spells, and item actives.

Press The Attack

Effects. Hitting an enemy champion with 3 consecutive basic attacks deals 40 - 180 (based on level) bonus Adaptive damage and makes them Vulnerable, increasing the damage they take by 8% from all sources for 6 seconds (The increased damage starts after the damage from the attack that applies it).

Overheal

Converts 20−100% of the healing in excess of your maximum health into a shield for an amount of up to 11% of your maximum health (depending on level). After 6 seconds, the shield decays by 1% of its maximum value every 0.25 seconds.

Triumph

Scoring a takedown against an enemy champion heals you for 2.5% of your maximum health (+5% of your missing health) and grants you an additional 20 gold.

Presence of Mind

Champion takedowns, after a 1-second delay, restore 15% of your maximum mana or 15% of your maximum energy. Damaging an enemy champion increases your mana regeneration by 1.5−11 mana per second (depending on level), reduced to 80% effectiveness for ranged champions, for 4 seconds.

Legend: Alacrity

Gain 3% attack speed plus an additional 1.5% for every Legend stack (max 10 stacks). Legend: Gain Legend stacks for every 100 points earned, up to 10: 100 points for champion takedowns. 100 points for epic monster takedowns. 25 points for large monster kills.

Legend: Tenacity

Gain 5% tenacity plus an additional 1.5% for every Legend stack (max 10 stacks). Legend: Gain Legend stacks for every 100 points earned, up to 10: 100 points for champion takedowns. 100 points for epic monster takedowns.

Legend: Bloodline

Gain 0.35% life steal per Legend stack, up to 5.25% at maximum stacks, at which you also gain 85 bonus health. Legend: Gain Legend stacks for every 100 points earned, up to 15: 100 points for champion takedowns.

Coup de Grace

Effects. Deal 8% more damage to champions who have less than 40% health. This does not apply to true damage.

Cut Down

Cut Down increases your damage output by 0% − 15% (based on target's additional maximum health). The formula to determine damage increase is (x - 0.1) × 1/9 + 0.05 where x is the additional maximum health that the target has in relation to the user of the rune.

Last Stand

Effects. Deal 5% bonus damage while below 60% of your maximum health. This increases based on your missing health, up to 11% bonus damage while below 30% of your maximum health.

Domination

Electrocute

Applying 3 stacks to a target within a 3 second period causes them to be struck by lightning after a 0.25-second delay, dealing them 30 − 180 (based on level) (+ 40% bonus AD) (+ 25% AP) Adaptive damage.

Predator

Gain bonus movement speed for 10 seconds, ramping up to 25-50% (levels 7-18) over 1.5 seconds, while moving toward enemy champions (3000 range, includes enemy champions out of vision). Activating Predator will not break stealth.

Dark Harvest

Damaging a champion below 50% of their maximum health deals 20-80 (+5 per Soul) (+5% of ability power) (+0.1 per bonus attack damage) bonus Adaptive damage and reaps 1 Soul (depending on level). This cannot happen again for 45 seconds, resetting to 1.5 seconds on champion takedown.

Hail of Blades

Basic attacking an enemy champion grants 110% bonus Attack Speed for your next 3 basic attacks. While active, Hails of Blade removes the attack speed cap. Hail of Blades lasts 3 seconds, and each basic attack refreshes the duration to 2 seconds.

Cheap Shot

Basic attacks and damaging abilities deal 10 - 45 (based on level) bonus true damage to slowed or immobilized enemy champions. Note that attacks or abilities that impair the target will not trigger that bonus damage themselves, unless the target is already slowed or immobilized.

Tast of Blood

Heal for 16 - 40 (based on level) ( Adaptive ratio: +10% of Bonus Attack Damage or +5% of Ability Power) the next time you damage an enemy champion. Cooldown: 20 seconds.

Sudden Impact

After exiting stealth or using a dash, blink or teleport, dealing any damage to a champion grants you 9 Lethality and 7 Magic Penetration for 5 seconds. The triggering damage will also benefit from the penetration.

Zombie Ward

Zombie Wards grant sight over 1100 units, are visible to the enemy team, have 1 health, last for 120 seconds and don't count towards your ward limit. Killing a Zombie Ward will grant 1 gold.

Ghost Poro

When your wards expire, they leave behind a Ghost Poro, which is untargetable and grants sight over a 450-unit area for 90 seconds. Wards killed by enemies or replacement do not spawn Ghost Poros.

Eyeball Collection

Collect eyeballs for champion takedowns. Gain an Adaptive bonus of 0.6 Attack Damage or 1 Ability Power, per eyeball collected. Upon completing your collection at 20 eyeballs, additionally gain an Adaptive bonus of 6 Attack Damage, or 10 Ability Power for a total of 18 Attack Damage, or 30 Ability Power.

Treasure Hunter

Gain 50 Gold (+ 20 Gold per Bounty Hunter stack) per unique takedown, up to 450 Gold at 5 unique takedowns. 1st unique takedown: 50 Gold , 2nd: 70 Gold , 3rd: 90 Gold , 4th: 110 Gold , 5th: 130 Gold .

Ignenious hunter

Gain 20 (+6 per Bounty Hunter stack) item haste, up to 50 at 5 stacks. Earn a Bounty Hunter stack whenever scoring a takedown against an enemy champion, up to one per unique champion.

Relentless Hunter

Gain 5 out-of-combat movement speed (+8 per Bounty Hunter stack), up to 45 at 5 stacks. Earn a Bounty Hunter stack for takedowns, up to one per unique enemy champion.

Ultimate Hunter

Your ultimate gains 5% reduced cooldown, plus an additional 4% per Bounty Hunter stack (max 25%). Earn a Bounty Hunter stack the first time you get a takedown on each enemy champion.

Sorcery

Summon Aery

Abilities targeted at allied champions signal Aery to dash to them, shielding them for 30 - 100 (based on level) (+5%) (+10% Bonus). Aery lingers on the target for 2 seconds before flying back to you, and cannot be sent out again until she returns to you.

Arcane Comet

Arcane Comet can be seen flying from the champion using it even if there is no vision of them, possibly giving away their position. The impact indicator has a radius of 175 units, for reference. The comet will be disabled on death, but its cooldown will still be able to get reduced by ability hits.

Phase Rush

Hitting an enemy champion with 3 attacks or separate abilities within 4 seconds grants ranged champions 15-40% movement speed, which is increased to 25-50% for melee champions (depending on level) and 75% slow resistance for the duration.

Nullifying Orb

When you take magic damage that would reduce your health below 30%, gain a shield that absorbs 35 - 110 (based on level) (+9%) (+14% Bonus) for 4 seconds. 60 seconds cooldown.

Manaflow Band

Hitting enemy champions with a spell grants 25 maximum Mana, up to 250 Mana. Upon earning 250 Mana, Manaflow Band restores 1% of your missing Mana every 5 seconds. 15 seconds cooldown.

Nimbus Cloak

Casting a summoner spell grants the ability to pass through units and provides a bonus of 5 - 35% movement speed (based on summoner spell cooldown) for 2 seconds. Cooldown: 60 seconds, reduced by Cooldown Reduction.

Trandscendence

Gain 10% cooldown reduction at level 10. Additionally, you gain either 2 Ability Power or 1. 2 bonus Attack Damage (Adaptive) for every 1% cooldown reduction in excess of the cap.

Celerity

Grants multiplicative bonus movement speed equal to 7% of the sum of your other multiplicative bonuses. On every new stat update, the multiplicative bonus of Celerity will avoid multiplying itself from the previous cycle.

Absolute Focus

Gain 1. 8 − 18 (based on level) bonus Attack Damage or 3 − 30 (based on level) Ability Power (Adaptive) while above 70% of your maximum health. Adaptive: Grants bonuses based on which stat you already have the most bonuses for.

Scorch

Your next damaging ability hit sets champions on fire, dealing 20 - 40 bonus magic damage based on level after 1 second.

Waterwalking

Gain 25 bonus movement speed and an Adaptive bonus of 3 - 18 (based on level) bonus Attack Damage or 5 - 30 (based on level) Ability Power when in the river.

Gathering Storm

Every 10 minutes, gain (1 per 10 Minutes) stacks of Gathering Storm. Each stack grants 8 ability power or 4. 8 attack damage (Adaptive). This stacks with no upper limit, for a total of 8 − ∞ (based on Minutes) AP or 5 − ∞ (based on Minutes) AD at 10 − ∞ (based on Stacks) minutes.

Inspiration

Glacial Augment

Immobilizing an enemy champion will cause 3 glacial rays to emanate from them towards you and other nearby enemy champions, creating icy zones that last for 3 (+100% of the immobilizing effect's duration) seconds.

Unsealed Spellbood

While being out-of-combat for 5 seconds, swap one of your equipped summoner spells to a new, single use summoner spell. You cannot select a summoner spell you have already equipped. Your first swap becomes available at the 6 minute mark, with an initial 240 second cooldown.

First Strike

Dealing damage to an enemy champion within the first 0.25 seconds of champion combat, grants 5 gold and First Strike for 3 seconds, causing all of your damage dealt against champions to deal 7% bonus damage.

Hextech Flashtrpation

While Flash is on cooldown, it is replaced by Hexflash. Hexflash: Channel for up to 2 seconds to blink to a new location. Uses Quick Casting by default, which requires the First Cast to be held and the Second Cast occurs on release.

Magical Footwear

You get free Slightly Magical Boots at 12 minutes, but you cannot buy boots before then. For each takedown you acquire the boots 45 seconds sooner. Slightly Magical Boots give you an additional +10 Movement Speed. This rune has no effect on Cassiopeia.

Perfect Timing

Perfect Timing gives you a Stopwatch worth 250 gold and no bonus. Only benefit is it gives you stopwatch early that normally costs 650 gold, but it only reduces 250 gold from Zhonya, making it less gold efficient than Magical Footwear.

Future's Market

You can enter debt to buy items, up to 150 gold (+5 gold per Minute). A 50 gold lending fee is added on to the amount of money you owe after leaving the store. Debt doesn't become available until 2 minutes into the game.

Minion Dematerializer

Minion Dematerializer that kill and absorb lane minions instantly, granting its user gold and experience normally. Minion Dematerializers are on cooldown for the first 180 seconds of the game, have 550 range, and are placed on a 10 seconds cooldown each time the user consumes one of them.

Biscuit Delivery

Gain a Total Biscuit of Everlasting Will every 3 mins, until 12 min. Biscuits restore 15% of your missing health and mana. Consuming any Biscuit increases your mana cap by 40 mana permanently. Manaless: Champions without mana restore 20% missing health instead.

Cosmic Insight

Gain 15 summoner spell haste and 10 item haste

Apporach Velocity

Gain 7.5% bonus movement speed when moving toward a movement-impaired enemy champion. Increased to 15% if you were the one who impaired their movement.

Time Warp Tonic

Consuming a potion or biscuit grants 30% of its health immediately, and mana if applicable. Health/mana per tick is then reduced by half for the effect's regular duration. If consumables are stacked, the instant restoration is applied after the duration of the initial consumable ends.

Resolve

Grasp of the Undying

If you have dealt or received damage in the last 2 seconds, you will generate stacks of Grasp of the Undying every second. At 4 stacks, your next attack against an enemy champion will deal 4% of your maximum health bonus magic damage, restore 2% of your maximum health, and permanently grants 5 maximum health.

Aftershock

After immobilizing an enemy champion, increase your Armor and Magic Resists by 35 + 80% of your Bonus Resists for 2.5 seconds. Then explode, dealing 25 - 120 (based on level) [+8% bonus] magic damage to nearby enemies. 20 seconds Cooldown. Resistance bonus from Aftershock capped at: 80 -150 based on level.

Guardian

While within 350 units of an allied Champion icon champion, you raise your Guard. Allied champions you affect with unit-targeted abilities are also Guarded for 3 seconds, regardless of distance. If you or a Guarded ally would take 90 − 250 (based on level) damage within 2.5 seconds or lethal damage from an enemy champion, Monster icon monster or Turret icon turret, you both gain a Hybrid resistances icon shield for 45 − 120 (based on level) (+ 12.5% of Guardian's AP) (+ 8% of Guardian's bonus health) for 2 seconds.

Demolish

You generate stacks on enemy structures within 600 units, up to 6 after 3 seconds. Stacks fall off slowly if you move out of range. Your next attack versus a structure with 6 stacks deals 100 [+35% your Max] bonus physical damage.

Font of Life

Impairing the movement of an enemy champion marks them for 4 seconds. Allied champions who attack marked enemies heal for 5 (+0.9% of your max health) over 2 seconds.

Shield Bash

Effects. While shielded, gain 1 - 10 bonus Armor and Magic Resist based on Level. Whenever you gain a new shield, your next basic attack against a champion deals 5 - 30 [+1.5% bonus] (+8.5% New Shield Amount) bonus adaptive damage. You have up to 2s after the shield expires to use this effect.

Conditioning

After 12 minutes gain 9 bonus Armor and 9 bonus Magic Resist and increases your total Armor and Magic Resists by 4%.

Second Wind

grants health regeneration equal to 1. 5 (+ 2% of missing health). If attacked while already under the effect of Second Wind, the duration will refresh. 36 × 3 health regen per 5s.

Bone Plating

After taking damage from an enemy champion, for 1.5 seconds, the next 3 spells or attacks you receive from the same enemy champion deal 30 − 60 (based on level) less damage (including. true damage).

Overgrowth

Absorb life essence from monsters or enemy minions that die near you (1400 range), permanently gaining 3 maximum Health for every 8. When you absorbed 120 monsters or enemy minions, gain an additional 3.5% maximum Health.

Revitalize

Grants 5% Heal and Shield Power. Outgoing healing and outgoing shielding is increased by 10% versus targets below 40% health. Incoming healing, incoming shielding, self healing and self shielding are increased by 10% while you are below 40% health.

Unflinching

Gain 5% slow resist and tenacity. This increases based on your missing health, up to 25% slow resist and tenacity at 30% maximum health or below.
